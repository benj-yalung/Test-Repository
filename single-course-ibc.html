Start Date: 10/12/2023

M, W, and F

Course Description

This course will build upon what students have learned in the first course while introducing more complex grammar structures. Students will learn how to not only talk about their day-to-day life but also be able to talk about the past and future. This course will also focus more on the business and professional world giving students the opportunity to practice the language in these contexts. Students will also learn about essential business culture in the western world.
